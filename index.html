Sign Up Online

No appointment required. Sign up, then fill out your personal infomation. Browse through your dashboard and wait for recommended strains.

Get Connected

You will get connected to our licensed doctor to buy, possess, use and cultivate medical-marijuana. All the documents will be sent by Email within 2 business days.

Shop
